Applied economist studying how public policies, institutions, and market-based mechanisms shape environmental outcomes. My research examines how environmental policies can be designed more effectively, from institutional design and environmental governance to market-based incentives, and how the transition to a sustainable economy can be achieved equitably. I use modern empirical methods to identify the causal effects of public policies.
